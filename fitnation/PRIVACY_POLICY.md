# Privacy Policy for Pulse

**Last Updated:** June 12, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Pulse ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in a transparent and secure manner, in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Union, relevant US privacy laws, and Google's policies.

## 1. Data We Collect

We collect various types of personal data to provide and improve our Service. The data we collect includes:

*   **Account and Profile Information:**
    *   Full Name
    *   Email Address
    *   Age
    *   Gender
    *   Weight
    *   Height
    *   Fitness Goals

*   **Workout and Activity Data:**
    *   Details of planned exercises
    *   Records of completed workouts, including sets, repetitions, weights, and duration
    *   Workout history and progress over time

*   **Meal Plan Data:**
    *   Information related to your meal plans, which may include dietary preferences and meal choices.

*   **Community Interaction Data:**
    *   User-generated content such as posts, comments, and reactions within the app's community features.
    *   Data related to workout or challenge posts.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected personal data solely for the following primary purposes related to providing core app functionality:

*   **Service Provision:** To operate, maintain, and provide all features and functionalities of the Pulse app.
*   **Personalization:** To personalize your experience, including generating customized workout and meal plans based on your fitness goals, weight, height, and workout history.
*   **Progress Tracking:** To help you track your fitness progress, analyze your performance, and visualize your achievements.
*   **Community Features:** To enable and enhance social interactions within the app, such as posting, commenting, and reacting to content.
*   **Communication:** To send you important updates, notifications, and information related to your account and the Service.

## 3. How We Share Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for their marketing or advertising purposes.

We share certain data with Google's Gemini API for the specific purpose of generating personalized workout and meal plans. This involves sending your fitness goals and workout history to the Gemini API. Google processes this data according to its data handling policies for API services, which typically involve anonymization and non-retention of data for service improvement. We ensure that any data shared with third-party services is done so with appropriate safeguards and in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your Pulse account remains active. If you choose to delete your account, we will retain your data for a period of 90 days after the account deletion date. This retention period allows for recovery in case of accidental deletion and for compliance with legal obligations. After this period, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

## 5. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

*   **Right to Access:** You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
*   **Right to Rectification:** You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or complete information you believe is incomplete.
*   **Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten):** You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
*   **Right to Restrict Processing:** You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
*   **Right to Object to Processing:** You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
*   **Right to Data Portability:** You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

You can manage much of your personal data directly through the in-app settings. To exercise any of these rights, or if you have questions about your data, please contact us at `pulse@gmail.com`.

## 6. Security of Your Data

We are committed to protecting the security of your personal data. We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. These measures include:

*   **Encryption:** Using encryption for data both in transit (e.g., SSL/TLS) and at rest.
*   **Access Controls:** Implementing strict access controls to limit who can access your personal data.
*   **Regular Security Audits:** Conducting regular security audits and vulnerability assessments to identify and address potential weaknesses.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Pulse app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
